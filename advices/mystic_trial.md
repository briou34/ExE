# Mystic Trial

Base troop ratio for Mystic Trial:

- 50-10-40 角鬥賽場 Coliseum
- 50-15-35 生命森林 Forest of Life
- 60-20-20 水晶礦洞 Crystal Cave
- 50-20-30 知識樞紐 Knowledge Nexus
- 60-15-25 熔岩要塞 Molten Fort
- 50-15-35 輝光尖塔 Radiant Spire

after defeat, check reports and adjust:

- Archery kills Infantry
- Infantry kills Cavalry
- Cavalry kills Archery

Can also adapt heroes formation, for example: if first formation is low on Cavalry ratio, then put main Cavalry hero in the second formation and increase Cavalry ratio in the second formation.
